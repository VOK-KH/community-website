     • Glow Effects (.glow-primary, .glow-secondary, .text-glow-*)
     • Hover Effects (.hover-lift, .hover-glow)
     • Border Gradients (.border-gradient)

  🎬 ANIMATIONS
     • .animate-float (3s infinite)
     • .animate-pulse-glow (2s infinite)
     • .animate-slide-in (0.5s)
     • .animate-fade-in (0.5s)
     • Timing: 150ms (fast) → 300ms (normal) → 500ms (slow)
     • Easing: ease-out, ease-in, ease-in-out
